Ground Beef Surprise

Description

This is a meal is simple to prepare and great for keto dieters.

Ingredients

  • one pound of ground beef
  • salt
  • spinach
  • crushed red peper

Steps

  1. Begin to brown ground beef in a skillet.
  2. Add salt and crushed red pepper while continuing to brown the beef.
  3. Mix spinach in to prevent constipation.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
  5. SUPRISE: This meal is disgusting and not for the faint of heart!
