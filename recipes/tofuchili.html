Tofu Chili

Description

This is a meal, both delicious and healthy, which you and your loved ones will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • tofu
  • chickpeas
  • kidney beans
  • tomato sauce
  • McCormick's Hot Chili Spice Packet
  • optional: curry powder
  • definitely optional: ghost peppers

Steps

  1. Put a tab of oil into the pan and allow to simmer.
  2. Then place the frozen block of chili into the sizzling oil. Break apart with a wooden spoon.
  3. Once the tofu has crumbled, apply all spices/peppers and stir.
  4. Then add tomato ingredients.
  5. Finally add the legumes and lower the heat.
  6. Let simmer for one hour while stirring occassionally.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
