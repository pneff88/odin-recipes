Lentils

Description

This is a meal is simple and cheap to prepare, not to mention nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients

  • one pound of lentils
  • salt
  • curry powder
  • garlic powder
  • water

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a rolling boil in a large pot.
  2. Place the lentils into the pot.
  3. Add spices and stir.
  4. Stir occassionally as water is absorbed by the lentils or evaporates over about twenty minutes.
  5. Allow to cool, then serve and enjoy
